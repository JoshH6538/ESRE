Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Equity Smart Real Estate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, and phone number if you voluntarily provide it through forms or contact submissions. We also use localStorage for UI settings, but do not automatically track or transmit any personal data unless you choose to share it with us.

2. Use of Your Information

The information you provide is used to respond to your inquiries and deliver requested services. We may also use it to inform you of updates, offerings, or real estate services provided by Equity Smart Real Estate.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with service providers only as necessary to fulfill your requests or support site operations, and those parties are required to keep your information confidential. Text messaging opt-in data is never shared.

4. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect browser information such as IP address, browser type, and referring URLs to improve the functionality and security of the site. No personally identifiable tracking cookies or analytics tools are used.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use browser-based localStorage to save UI settings. We do not use traditional cookies or tracking software. You can clear your localStorage data through your browser settings.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including encryption and SSL, to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Right to Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data, subject to exceptions such as legal obligations, fraud prevention, and transaction fulfillment. Requests can be made by contacting us directly.

8. Children Under 13

We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13. If you are under 13, please use the site only with the consent of a parent or guardian.

9. Email Communication

We may occasionally email you with announcements or offers. You may opt out at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any message or replying STOP.

10. Data Storage

Your data may be stored on secure third-party servers used for hosting or delivery purposes. These vendors are under contract to maintain data security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

For questions or requests related to this Privacy Policy, email ithelp@equitysmartloans.com or visit our Contact page.
